Does LPU have an innovation lab?

Yes, LPU has an innovation lab. Being alumni of LPU, I can share details about it with you. LPU believes more in practical and deep knowledge of subjects than theoretical. In my opinion, a student can do theory but in practicals we need guidance. LPU provides professional experience to undergraduates and graduate students related to space research. Starting from the installation of the ground station, HAM installation, satellite tracking till data set collection each step is defined step by step. The whole training has different 6 modules. The main concern of the program is to train the next generation of engineers, to meet future research and development challenges in space research. These include technical and professional development training, presenting their scientific work. The research projects would be used for future space missions The main motive is to equip LPU-Students with space data and open a field of research in Satellite and Satellite Tracking systems, Satellite Tracking and Data Acquisition, Antenna Designing and Simulation, Laser/optical lab for futuristic Communication Systems. LPU has its own fully-fledged Apple IMac lab, CISCO lab which provides exposure to the latest software as well as the development of advanced projects for I- Mac machines. The following are some of the labs available at LPU: iMac Lab Fully equipped ultra modest Apple IMac lab provides exposure to the latest software as well as the development of advanced projects for I- Mac machines. Industrial Automation And Robotics Lab The lab is designed for exposure in the field of automation and robotics by working on various pneumatic systems and CNC machines. Digital VLSI Design Lab State-of-the-art Industry version EDA Tools from Cadence Design Systems, Mentor Graphics, and Xilinx & Altera FPGA Development Kits, Verilog, VHDL Design & Simulation Tools. Check more out on the official website of LPU. For more details, you may refer to the LPU website.
